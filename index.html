Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The man who saved his country

Here's a time line of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk's life:

  • 1988 - Born in Selanik, Greece
  • 1893 - Mustafa enters military preparatory school in Salonica.
  • 1903 - Promoted First Lieutenant.
  • 1907 - Promoted Adjutant-Major and Posted to Third Army headquarters in Salonica.
  • 1911 - Posted to general staff in Constantinople.
  • 1912 - Appointed director of operations of Straits Composite Force.
  • 1913 - Mustafa Kemal promoted Lieutenant-Colonel.
  • 1914 - Mustafa enters military preparatory school in Salonica.
  • 1915 - Mustafa Kemal leaves Sofia to take up appointment as commander of 19th division for service in Battle of Gallipoli.
  • 1919 - Mustafa Kemal resigns from the post of Inspector of Third Army and from the army. Kemal elected Chairman of Erzurum Congress.
  • 1920 - Appointed Commander - in - Chief by the Grand National Assembly.
  • 1921 - The battle of Sakarya begins with Turkish troops led by Mustafa Kemal. Kemal receives the rank of Marshal and the title Gazi
  • 1922 - Gazi Mustafa Kemal begins to lead Great Offensive from the hill of Kocatepe.
  • 1923 - Proclamation of the Republic of Turkey. Elected first president.
  • 1928 - Speaks at Sarayburnu about the new Turkish alphabet.
  • 1938 - He dies.
